<h2>Learning Through Play, Exploration, and Expression</h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Every day offers opportunities for children to explore, create, and collaborate. Our classrooms are thoughtfully arranged to encourage independence and joyful engagement. Children participate in:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><b>Artistic expression</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> through drawing, painting, sculpting, and imaginative play</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><b>Hands-on math and science</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> activities that spark early reasoning and problem‑solving</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><b>Music and movement</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> that develop rhythm, coordination, and body awareness</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><b>Storytelling and early literacy</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> experiences that expand vocabulary and ignite a love of reading</span></li>
</ul>

<h2>Building Autonomy and Essential Life Skills</h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As children grow, so does their desire to do things “all by myself.” We honor this developmental leap by guiding them through essential self-care routines, including </span><b>apprentissage de la propreté</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> (potty training), dressing, tidying, and managing personal belongings. <h2>Social-Emotional Growth and Character Development</h2>

<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><b>Respect (respect)</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> for themselves, their peers, and their environment</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><b>Cooperation (coopération)</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> as they work together toward common goals</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><b>Patience (patience)</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> in taking turns and listening to others</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><b>Kindness (gentillesse)</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> in words, actions, and problem‑solving</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">These values are woven into daily routines, helping children build meaningful friendships and navigate social interactions with confidence and empathy.</span></p>
<h2>Preparing for the Next Chapter</h2>
